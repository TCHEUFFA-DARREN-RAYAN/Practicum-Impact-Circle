# Volunteer User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Go to `/register-volunteer` and create your account.
2. Complete your profile: upload your government ID and fill in your skills, interests, and availability.
3. Wait for admin verification (you will receive an email once approved).

## Browsing Gigs

- Visit `/upcoming-shifts` to browse available opportunities.
- Use filters:
  - **Time of Day** — morning, afternoon, or evening shifts
  - **Hours** — filter by how many hours you can commit (Under 2h / 2–4h / 4–8h / 8+h)
  - **Category** — filter by cause area
  - **Search** — keyword search across titles and descriptions

## Applying for a Gig

1. Click **View Details** on any gig card.
2. If your account is verified, click **Apply Now**.
3. Write your personal statement (minimum 20 characters) and submit.
4. The Apply button will change to **Waiting for Approval** — you cannot apply twice.
5. You will receive an email and in-app notification when a decision is made.

## Task Workflow

Once your application is approved, a Task is created in your dashboard.

| Button | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Start Task** | Begin the gig — moves status to In Progress |
| **Claim Hours** | Confirm you completed the work — submits for org approval |
| Awaiting approval | Org has up to 30 days to verify; auto-approved after that |

## Hours & Points

- Approved tasks add verified hours to your profile.
- Points are awarded based on the category's points-per-hour rate.
- Redeem points for rewards at `/rewards`.

## Dashboard

Your dashboard (`/volunteer-dashboard`) shows:
- Verified hours, total gigs applied, gigs completed, active tasks
- Live task list with action buttons
- Hours breakdown by cause category
- Available rewards to redeem
