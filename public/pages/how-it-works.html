How It Works

Getting started takes less than five minutes — whether you want to give back as a volunteer or find dedicated help for your organization.

Volunteers

Four steps to your first shift

No experience required. Just a willingness to show up and make a difference.

1

Create Your Profile

Register as a volunteer, list your skills and availability, and let organizations know what you bring to the table. Takes about two minutes.

2

Browse Verified Opportunities

Explore shifts filtered by cause area, time slot, location, or skill needed. Every listing comes from a verified organization — no spam, no surprises.

3

Apply & Get Confirmed

Send your application with a single click. The organization reviews your profile, confirms the match, and the shift appears in your personal schedule.

4

Volunteer & Track Your Hours

Complete your shift, claim your hours through the dashboard, and watch your verified impact record grow over time. Your contributions are permanent and shareable.

Organizations

Recruit reliable volunteers in four steps

Stop relying on email lists and spreadsheets. Manage everything in one place.

1

Register Your Organization

Create an organization profile and submit it for verification. Once approved, your badge lets volunteers know you're a trusted, legitimate partner.

2

Post Volunteer Shifts

List one-time or recurring opportunities with details on timing, location, required skills, and how many volunteers you need per shift.

3

Review & Approve Applicants

Browse volunteer profiles, approve the best matches, and send messages directly — all from your organization dashboard.

4

Track & Report Your Impact

Approve volunteer hours, generate downloadable impact reports, and demonstrate the community value your organization is creating.

Ready to get started?

Joining is free. Your first shift is just a few clicks away.
