How It Works

Getting started is simple — whether you're a volunteer looking to give back or an organization seeking dedicated help.

For Volunteers

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up, add your skills and availability, and let organizations know what you bring to the table.

2

Browse Opportunities

Explore verified shifts by category, location, time of day, or cause area that resonates with you.

3

Apply & Get Matched

Apply for shifts that fit your schedule. Organizations review your profile and confirm the match.

4

Volunteer & Track Impact

Show up, complete your shift, claim your hours, and watch your impact grow on your personal dashboard.

For Organizations

1

Register Your Organization

Create a verified organization profile so volunteers know you're a legitimate, trusted partner.

2

Post Opportunities

List your volunteer shifts with details on time, location, skills needed, and how many people you need.

3

Review Applicants

See who applied, review their profiles, approve the right volunteers, and communicate directly.

4

Measure Your Impact

Track volunteer hours, generate impact reports, and celebrate the community difference you're making.
