About ImpactCircle

We believe every person has something meaningful to give. Our platform turns that belief into action — connecting volunteers and organizations across Greater Moncton.

Our Mission

ImpactCircle is a verified volunteer and community impact exchange platform built for Greater Moncton and surrounding areas. We make it effortless for individuals to find meaningful service opportunities, for organizations to recruit dedicated volunteers, and for communities to measure and celebrate real impact.

Every hour volunteered on our platform is verified, recorded, and counted — building a public record of the good happening in our region.

  • Verified organizations and volunteer profiles
  • Real-time matching by skill, schedule, and cause
  • Transparent impact tracking for volunteers and orgs

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every decision we make — from platform design to community partnerships.

Community First

Every feature we build starts with a single question: does this make community volunteering more accessible, rewarding, and impactful?

Verified & Trusted

We manually verify organizations before they can post opportunities. Volunteers can trust that every shift listed is legitimate, safe, and well-managed.

Measurable Impact

We track hours, milestones, and community outcomes so every act of service gets the recognition it deserves — for the volunteer and the org.

Inclusive Access

From accessible scheduling to bilingual support, we design for everyone — including those volunteering for the first time.

Community in Action

From food banks and youth mentoring to environmental cleanups and senior support programs, volunteers on ImpactCircle are making a difference across dozens of cause areas every single week.

Since launching, our community has logged thousands of verified hours spanning everything from tutoring students after school to sorting donations at local shelters and planting trees in city parks.

Whether you have two hours a month or two days a week, there is a place for you here.
