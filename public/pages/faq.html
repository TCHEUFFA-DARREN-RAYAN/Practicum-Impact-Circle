Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about ImpactCircle.

General
ImpactCircle is a verified volunteer and community impact exchange platform. We connect individuals who want to volunteer with verified organizations that need their help.
Yes, ImpactCircle is completely free for volunteers. Organizations may have access to premium features in the future, but the core platform is free for everyone.
Simply click "Register" to create your account. Choose whether you're signing up as a volunteer or an organization, complete your profile, and start exploring opportunities or posting shifts.
For Volunteers
Browse Upcoming Shifts to see all available opportunities. You can filter by category, time of day, location, and type (in-person or remote).
Click "Apply" on any shift you're interested in. The organization will review your application and either approve or decline it. Once approved, the shift will appear in your schedule.
After completing a shift, you can claim your hours through your dashboard. The organization then verifies and approves your claimed hours, which are added to your impact record.
For Organizations
After registering, our admin team reviews your organization details. Once verified, you'll receive a badge on your profile and full access to post opportunities and manage volunteers.
Yes! When posting an opportunity, you can set it as recurring (daily, weekly, etc.) with a set end date. The system automatically manages scheduling and sign-ups for each occurrence.
Your organization dashboard shows all approved volunteers for each shift. Volunteers can claim their hours, and you can approve or mark attendance directly from the dashboard.
