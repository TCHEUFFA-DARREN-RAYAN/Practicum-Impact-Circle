About ImpactCircle

Trusted, Verified Volunteering for Greater Moncton

ImpactCircle was built to solve a fundamental problem in volunteering: the inability to verify and measure community contributions. We created a platform where every hour is approved by the hosting organization — not self-reported.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be verified to volunteer?
No — you can apply to most gigs as soon as you register. However, some organizations post verified-only gigs that require your Verified Volunteer badge. Getting verified is free and unlocks exclusive high-impact opportunities.
How are my volunteer hours tracked and approved?
After you complete a task, you mark it done in the platform. The hosting organization receives a notification and has 30 days to approve or reject. If they don't respond within 30 days, the task is automatically approved and hours are credited to you.
What kinds of gigs are available?
Gigs span 8 community impact categories: Food Security, Women Support, Youth Development, Seniors Support, Environment, Education, Newcomer Integration, and general Volunteer Opportunities. You can filter by category, location, date, or organization.
How does the points and rewards system work?
You earn points for every verified hour based on the category's points-per-hour rate. You can then redeem points for rewards sponsored by CSR partners — including discounts, event passes, recognition badges, and certificates of service. Points are never self-reported; they are always tied to approved hours.
Can organizations post volunteer opportunities?
Yes. Non-profit organizations register separately, go through a verification process, and can then post gigs, screen applicants, and verify task completion. Organizations get a dedicated dashboard showing all their gig activity and volunteer metrics.
Is ImpactCircle free to use?
Yes — ImpactCircle is completely free for volunteers and non-profit organizations. The platform is sustained through CSR partnerships with private sector companies who sponsor the rewards catalogue in exchange for branded visibility and employee engagement tracking.
