About ImpactCircle

Built for Greater Moncton. Designed to Scale.

ImpactCircle was built to solve a fundamental problem in volunteering: the inability to verify and measure community contributions. Every hour is approved by the hosting organization — never self-reported.

Zero Tolerance for Unverified Hours Every hour requires explicit organizational approval before it counts.
